   - Should be: "error" → "erreur"

2. Line 43: "Conditions d'état" is awkward
   - Should be: "États" or "Statuts"

3. Line 110: "Conditions de commercialisation" inconsistent with other headers
   - Should be: "Termes de marketing" or "Terminologie marketing"
